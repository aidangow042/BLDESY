The Problem

Finding a reliable tradie in Australia is frustrating. Customers spend hours searching Google, asking friends, and calling around. Existing platforms like Hipages charge tradies per lead (driving up costs), while Airtasker focuses on odd jobs rather than licensed trade work.

Builders and tradies, meanwhile, waste money on pay-per-lead platforms where they compete in bidding wars, often losing jobs to the lowest quote rather than the best fit.

BLDESY! is a pure connector. No quote bidding. No pay-to-win rankings. Customers find the right tradie based on trade, location, urgency, and verified credentials. Builders pay a flat subscription to be listed — that's it.

Competitive Advantage

vs Hipages

No per-lead fees. Builders pay a flat subscription, so there's no incentive to race to the bottom on pricing.

vs Airtasker

Purpose-built for licensed trades, not odd jobs. Verified credentials (ABN, licence, insurance) build real trust.

vs Google Search

Structured profiles, verified reviews, urgency matching, and AI-powered recommendations — not just a list of links.

vs Word of Mouth

Searchable by trade + location + urgency. See credentials, past work, and reviews before making contact.

Revenue Model

Flat Subscription for Builders

Builders pay a monthly subscription to be listed on the platform. No commission, no per-lead fees. Tiers provide different levels of visibility and features.

Phase 4 (upcoming): Stripe integration for automated payments, subscription management, and tier-based features.
